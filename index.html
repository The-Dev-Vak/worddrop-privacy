WordDrop Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

No Data Collection

WordDrop does not collect, store, or share any personal information.

Local Storage Only

The app stores minimal data locally on your device:

  • High score
  • Sound preferences
  • Tutorial completion status
  • Premium purchase status

This data never leaves your device.

No Tracking

We do not use analytics, advertising trackers, or any third-party services that collect your data.

In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed securely through Apple's App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.
